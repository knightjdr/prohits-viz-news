July 18, 2021 was the last day of official support for our old ProHits-viz site hosted at prohits-viz.lunenfeld.ca. Over the course of its four years of existence, it performed over 75 000 analysis and visualization tasks. While it is still used extensively by the community, we felt it was in need of a redesign that would allow us to more easily add new tools and interactive features going forward.

ProHits-viz.org is the new redesigned home of ProHits-viz. This new site has an overhauled interface, with extensive online help and video tutorials to aid when you get stuck. It also comes with a brand new tool for visualizing multiple output metrics from a single experimental condition.

There is plenty of online help at prohits-viz.org/help and a number of videos that demonstrate how to use the new interactive viewers.

If you have questions that are not covered by the help, you can contact us at contact@prohits-viz.org

A final note on the old site: any images that were archived there will still be accessible, but all other links will redirect to the new site.
